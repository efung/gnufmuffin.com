I learned about two (2!) baking supply warehouses this week that were previously unknown to me:

  • David Roberts Food Corporation has an outlet store in Mississauga (Google Map). They have a huge assortment of packaged nuts (375 g tub of macadamia nuts for $8!), dried fruit, snacks, gift baskets and more.
  • McCall's Bakers Warehouse is also in Mississauga (Google map) and they are open to the public over the coming weekend and next (Dec 2, 3). They sell the same products as the McCall's store in Etobicoke, obviously: cake decorating supplies (boxes, icings, colourings, flavourings, decorations), cake tins, chocolate and candy making supplies. They even had chocolate sticks for making pain au chocolat.
