Recipe from Gordon Ramsay's Healthy Appetite

Notes: Continuing my search for oat recipes, I decided to try this muffin using quick-cooking oats. Unusually, it uses four bananas for a dozen muffins. To cut back on sweetness, I halved the quantity of brown sugar. While these turned out okay, they were a little bit gummy. Perhaps with old-fashioned rolled oats, or with fewer bananas, it would be better. I don't think I would make this again.
