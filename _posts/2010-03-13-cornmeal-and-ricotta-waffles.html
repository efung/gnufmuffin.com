Recipe by Michael Mina from Luxurious Christmas Weekend via Food and Wine
Notes: These waffles feature the inclusion of ricotta cheese which adds some protein (and fat), and also separate beating of yolks and whites. The addition of cornmeal adds a little crunch, but the crispiness comes from the whipped egg whites. And are these waffles ever crisp and light-tasting! Definitely would make again.

N.B.: All pictures of waffles look the same. There's really no value to me including a photo of a waffle. By the way, those little berries on top are wild strawberries from a bottle of Fragoli.
