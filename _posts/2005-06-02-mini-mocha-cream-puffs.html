This big sheet pan blocks air from circulating in my oven. As a result, this batch didn't puff too much.

Filled with whipped cream flavoured with chocolate and espresso powder.

 
Recipe adapted from The Secrets of Baking by Sherry Yard
Notes: Use a bit more paste for each puff, small ones are hard to fill. Use less espresso powder per cup of cream (half tbsp?). Unsweetened chocolate was not a good idea. Hewitt's Dairy makes whipping cream without additives.
